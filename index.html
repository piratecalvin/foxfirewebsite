Calvin Higby is a CISSP-certified security leader with 15 years of experience designing and operating security programs in two of the most demanding regulatory environments in the industry: defense contracting and financial services.

Most recently, Calvin served as Senior Security Manager at a major regional insurance carrier, where he led the enterprise security program across identity governance, threat detection and incident response, vulnerability management, cloud security, data protection, and compliance. He aligned the organization to NIST, SOC 2, and HIPAA simultaneously — establishing board-level reporting cadences, domain-driven ownership models, and measurable KPIs across the program. On the cloud and infrastructure side, he led the enterprise deployments of Wiz for AWS security posture management, Netskope for zero-trust web access, and Tenable for continuous vulnerability management.

Earlier in his career, Calvin spent years in defense contracting, working within DoD-regulated environments and building security programs aligned to federal standards including DoD 8570.

He founded FoxFire Security to give growing organizations access to that level of security leadership — without the cost of a full-time executive hire. He works directly with every client, from the first conversation through final delivery.

His current focus includes helping organizations develop structured governance programs for AI tools — an area where regulatory and insurance pressure is accelerating faster than most security teams can keep up.
